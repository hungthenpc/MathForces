MathForces is Now Live!
We are thrilled to announce the official launch of MathForces — a brand new platform for competitive mathematics. Explore our initial problem set covering topics from algebra and combinatorics to number theory, geometry, and beyond. With problems rated from 800 to 3500, there's something for everyone — from beginners to seasoned mathematicians.

This is just the beginning. We're actively working on adding more problems, contest features, user accounts, rankings, and a submission system. Stay tuned for updates and happy problem solving!
